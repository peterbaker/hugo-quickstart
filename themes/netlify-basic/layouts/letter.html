What can you do to help support clean, profitable energy in Lenawee County?

  • Get informed about the facts of solar energy.
  • Prepare yourself to try and find common ground with others, let them understand why you are doing this and the benefit that it offers for the community.
  • Collect letters of support from friends and family.
  • Build relationships with your Township Officials and Planning Commission Members by calling them and meeting with them to show them all the benefits of the project and why you are doing this.
  • Submit Letters to the Editor.
  • Organize your friends and family to attending public hearings and voicing support for the project.
